Necessary ingredients:

  • Meat
  • Onion and Garlic
  • Tomato sauce
  • Sugar
  • Spices and seasoning
  • Lasagna noodles
  • Cheeses
    • Parmesan
    • Mozzarella
    • Ricotta
  • Egg

Step-by-step instructions:

  1. Make the meat sauce
  2. Cook the noodles
  3. Make the ricotta mixture
  4. Layer the lasagna
    1. Meat Sauce
    2. Noodles
    3. Ricotta mixture
    4. Mozzarella slices
    5. Meat sauce
    6. Parmesan cheese
    7. Repeat the layers, then top with the remaining parmesan
  5. Cook the lasagna in the oven at 375 degrees F for two 50 minute splits, both covered in foil and uncovered
  6. Let the lasagna rest for 15 minutes at a room temperature
