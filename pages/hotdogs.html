Illustrative image for this recipe

Necessary ingredients:

  • 4 Hot dogs
  • 4 buns
  • Mustard
  • Ketchup
  • Toppings of choice

Step-by-step instructions

  1. Cook the hot dogs over medium heat on a grill, ocassionally turning them until they're heated internally and showing char on the outside
  2. Cook the buns until they're slightly crusty
  3. Once the hot dogs are cooked, remove from heat and dry them off if necessary
  4. Place each hot dog in a bun and top with desired toppings and condiments
