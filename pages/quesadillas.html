Quesadilla Recipe

Home

Illustrative image of this recipe

Necessary ingredients:

  • Tortiillas
  • 1/2 cup grated cheddar cheese
  • 1/4 cup black beans, rinsed and drained
  • Oil of your preference
  • Fillings of preference

Step-by-step instructions

  1. Use one tortilla add the grated cheddar cheese, then fold the tortilla over itself to create a half-moon shape
  2. Add a tablespoon of oil then cook the quesadillas to a moderate heat on a medium skillet until the tortilla has a golden color in its both sides
  3. While the quesadillas are cooking, simultaneously cook the black beans.
  4. When both the quesadillas and the beans are cooked, add the beans into your quesadillas and add the fillings
  5. Once everything is done, you can cut your quesadillas in smaller triangles
