Necessary ingredients:

  • 16 ounces pizza dough (homemade or store-bought)
  • 1/2 cup pizza sauce (homemade or store bought)

      Pizza Sauce ingredients:

    • 1/2 cup tomato sauce (no salt)
    • 1/2 teaspoon kosher salt
    • 1/2 teaspoon granulated garlic
    • 1/2 granulated onion
    • 1/4 teaspoon red pepper flakes
    • 1 teaspoon olive oil
  • 18 slices pepperoni
  • 12 ounces grated mozzarella cheese
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground black pepper
  • 1 teaspoon fresh oregano (optional)
  • Flour for rolling and shaping dough

  4. Add a light layer of sauce all over the pizza, be sure to leave 1/4 inch crust around the edges
  5. Slice half the pepperoni and add it along with the grated cheese on the pizza
  6. Season with black pepper and slide the pizza into the center of the oven
  7. Cook for 6 minutes, then rotate the pizza so it cooks evenly, afterwards, cook for another 6 minutes until the crust shows a golden color and char.
  8. Once cooked, slide the pizza into a cutting board and slice it into pieces
